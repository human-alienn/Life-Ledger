About Us

LifeLedger is a decentralized health record platform focused on resilience, privacy, and patient ownership. We aim to put healthcare control back in your hands.

Mission

LifeLedger is a global, blockchain-based medical record system that gives people permanent, private, and secure ownership of their health data. Designed for everyone, whether in advanced or developing healthcare systems, LifeLedger replaces fragile, fragmented records with a trusted, tamper-proof platform that travels with the patient.

Medical Services

We provide encrypted record storage, emergency access systems, and real-time doctor-patient communication to support modern digital healthcare delivery.
